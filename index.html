What is Demo Day?

Demo Day is the capstone project for All Star Code students and is featured at the end of the Summer Intensive. The Summer Intensive is a 6 week camp where students learn mobile web development as well as soft skills. By the end, students are capable of creating web applications from ideation to production along with the confidence making professional contacts and presenting their ideas to investors.
